One More Time.

Founded as a school for the instruction of Shotokan Karate, students also have the opportunity to learn from masters in Jiu-Jitsu, Iaido, and take part in specific self-defense courses derived from these arts.

Shotokan Karate is a school promoted by the teachings of Gichin Funakoshi, who is widely credited with the proliferation of Karate-do throughout Japan and the world. Justice Karate American Shotokan blends distinctly American martial techniques with Shotokan Karate. Since 1972 this synthesis has resulted in an award winning and practical style.

JKAS offers courses for all levels:

  • Justice Karate American Shotokan
  • Iaido
  • Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
  • Law Enforcement & Security Series
  • Foundations of Self-Defense
  • Aerobic Conditioning
